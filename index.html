Hi, I'm Glowtham

Professional photographer based in the Philippines, specialising in capturing the fleeting moments that define our most precious memories. My lens finds beauty in the extraordinary hidden within the everyday — from golden-hour light washing over city streets to the raw electricity of a live event.

With over five years crafting visuals across portraiture, landscape, and event photography, I bring a distinctive aesthetic that blends technical precision with raw emotional resonance. Every photograph should freeze time, evoke feeling, and tell a truth words cannot express.

I also built FireDL[Beta] — a modern file-hosting platform for seamless, secure sharing. Both projects reflect my passion for creating tools and art that genuinely serve people.
